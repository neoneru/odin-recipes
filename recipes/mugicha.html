Mugicha (Barley Tea)

Description

Mugicha is the kind of thing you crave on a hot day. It's a cooling elixir that gets you through the sweaty sauna Japan becomes in summer, or cools you down after a long afternoon of wandering around Disneyland in bone-dry Anaheim, California, throwing up peace signs with Mickey Mouse.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons roasted barley tea, or 1 barley tea bag

Method

  1. Put barley tea bag and cool or room temperature water in a pitcher or glass container.
  2. Cover pitcher and put in refrigerator for 1-2 hours.
  3. Discard tea bag and pour tea into a cup with ice.
